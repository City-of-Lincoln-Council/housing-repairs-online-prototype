Request a repair

Before you start

! Warning For emergency repairs call 0800 952 4444 or 020 7525 2600, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You may experience longer waiting times due to a high volume of calls.
What is an emergency?

An emergency repair is when there's immediate danger to you or the structure of the building. In an emergency we'll make the situation safe but we may need to return another day to complete the full repair.

You can use this service to request repairs to council properties.

To request a repair for a communal area call 0800 952 4444.
